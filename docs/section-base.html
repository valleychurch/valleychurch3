Typography

Default styles for typography across the site.

Defaults

By default, the base of the site is set to 1rem (equivalent to 16px) so we bump this up slightly to 17px on mobile and 19px on desktop to give everything a bit more legibility. We also give some defaults to ul/ol elements to make them match.

Lead paragraphs

Using the class .lead you can bump up a paragraph's size to 19px on mobile and 22px on desktop.

Headings

Blockquotes

For quoting blocks of content from another source within your document. Wrap <blockquote> around any HTML as the quote.

Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver. 

  <blockquote>
    <p>
      Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give,
      not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.
    </p>
  </blockquote>

Add a <footer> for identifying the source.

Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.

—2 Corinthians 9:7 
  <blockquote>
    <p>
      Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give,
      not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver.
    </p>
    <footer>
      &mdash;<a href="#0"><strong>2 Corinthians 9:7</strong></a>
    </footer>
  </blockquote>
