Tone and Voice

As a church everything we do aims to reflect the vision of creating a home to empower a new generation. The way we communicate through all of our different channels is incredibly important to provide a consistent message.

Voice

Our voice is warm, friendly, inviting and inclusive. Everything we write should communicate welcome home.

Grammar and Mechanics

Adhering to certain rules of grammar and mechanics helps us keep our writing clear and consistent.

Dates and times

Spell out the day of the week and the month, and include an ordinal (th, nd) unless you're just referring to the month. Times are always separated by a colon (:)

  • Sunday 31st January 2016
  • Mon 27th Mar 2016
  • July 2017

To separate dates or times, use an ndash (&ndash;, appears as –)

  • Monday–Friday
  • 6–7:30pm
